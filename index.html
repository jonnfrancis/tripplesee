Tripple See Limited is a company which is set up to foster green products in line with solid waste management and climate change mitigation. This are basically industrial products ranging from green energy materials, green industrial products, consultant and training services

Message From Us

Tripple See limited is dedicated to finding climate neutral affordable and sustainable technological solutions for converting waste biomass into renewable energy.

Waste biomass is one of the greatest menace in public, residential, domestic and urban areas. Especially wet biomass waste is a rapid versatile breeding ground for both disease vectors and contagious diseases.
The company is therefore to develop novel technologies for the conversion of waste biomass to energy and other useful industrial products.
This is a case of a symbiotic industrial phenomenon, where no waste is generated and everything becomes a product. A waste from one line of production becomes a row material for the other line of production. This is the spirit of zero waste and emissions to the environment. One could imagine a situation where the term waste biomass diminishes from the vocabulary and instead we talk of raw materials.
The developed technologies, procedures, innovations, inventions and products will go a long way to spur growth in rural and urban economics enhance environmental protection, improve livelihoods and sustainably reduce diseases.

Products

Briquettes

A briquette (French: [bʁikɛt]; also spelled briquet) is a compressed block of coal dust or other combustible biomass material (e.g. charcoal, sawdust, wood chips, peat, or paper) used for fuel and kindling to start a fire. The term derives from the French word brique, meaning brick. We deliver quality briquette fuels upon your request. The briquette fuels are available at a friendly cost.these are efficient fuels with high combustibility levels.
